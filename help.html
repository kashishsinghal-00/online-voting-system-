Help Center & Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Registration & Login

How do I register on the portal?
To register, click on "Create an account" on the login page. You will need to provide your full name, mobile number, and verify using either your Aadhar or Voter ID details.
I forgot my password. How can I reset it?
On the login page, click the "Forgot Password?" link and follow the instructions. You will typically need to verify your identity via mobile OTP or email.

Voting Process

How do I cast my vote online?
Once an election is active for you, log in to your dashboard. You will see an option to "View Candidates & Cast Vote". Follow the on-screen instructions carefully.
Is online voting secure?
We use multiple layers of security, including OTP verification and data encryption, to ensure the integrity and confidentiality of your vote.
